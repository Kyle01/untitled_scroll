Sedo code for painting Rothko
----

1) Pick a predefined color pallet
2) pick 2 or 3 sections
3) if 2
  pick a divider between 30 and 70
  draw circles of the pure color  with a 5px top and bottom buffer, 10px left and right bumper, and 5px inbetween
    draw a cricle outside of the median color
  gradient sides
4) if 3
  pick a divider between 10 and 20
  then split the other 2
  draw circles  with a 5px top and bottom buffer, 10px left and right bumper, and 3px inbetween
  gradient sides
